Password derivation:
1. apply bcrypt with salt '$2a$14$.12345678901234512345.'. i.e. 2^14 rounds, bcrypt result prefix as specified.
2. take SHA512 of result and convert to hex string

encryption is similar to
openssl enc -d -aes-256-cbc -pbkdf2 -iter 1 -md sha512 -pass pass:{key} -salt
to decrypt, use
  openssl enc -aes-256-cbc -pbkdf2 -iter 1 -md sha512 -pass pass:{key} -salt | gzip -d
